KATHNIEL'S IN 12 YEARS

12 years as a loveteam, and a successful one at that, KathNiel truly created a legacy in the industry. And now that we are celebrating 12 years in this fandom, we still cannot fathom how we created what we did. From fan projects, creating events to organizing movie screenings and donation

First Project (Starting LoveTeam)

They were part of the 2011 teen drama "Growing Up" which is about a group of friends who collectively discovers and understands valuable truths about love, family, relationships and life in general. But doing so proves to be no easy task, as each of them discover their respective strengths and weaknesses that challenge the way they think, dream, and love, and in so doing become obstacles to their objective. In the end, they manage to reap the reward they worked for, only to find out that it’s better than they hoped for. In one of her recent videos, the 25-year-old actress asked Daniel if he had any regrets on starting their relationship so young, as they had met when they were only 16 and 17, respectively. “Hindi ah,” Daniel answered firmly.

A Timeline of their Showbiz love

If there’s one couple that comes to mind when talking about the best modern showbiz pairings, it’s Kathryn Bernardo and Daniel Padilla. With their catchy shipname KathNiel and their opposites-attract personalities, these two have proven that it’s possible to grow up in a healthy and loving relationship while in showbiz. Recently, they celebrated their 10th anniversary in the industry and we’re here to look back on their momentous journey from teenhood to celebrated individual artists. Part of their movies & Teleserye are Growing Up (2011), Princess and I (2012), Must be Love and Pagpag (2013), She's Dating the Gangster (2014), Crazy Beautiful You (2015), Barcelona: A love Untold (2016), Can't Help Falling Inlove (2017), The Hows of Us (2018), The Hows Arresst of Us (2020).

Present KathNiel

They're now doing vlogs in Everyday Kath Channel and planning the future together. Though the two have been keeping low-key on projects amid the pandemic, they’re still regularly seen discussing their future acting plans in Kathryn’s vlog, Everyday Kath. Kathryn Bernardo admitted that she doesn't see herself getting married to her longtime boyfriend Daniel Padilla anytime soon, as she clarified that they want to focus on their respective priorities at the moment. And now, they manage to set their self in new surrounding for the part of their growth and self reflection. Kath and Daniel has their own single project; A Very Good Girl for Kath and Daniel are still working on it.

Must be Love
is a 2013 Filipino coming-of-age romantic comedy film directed by Dado Lumibao, starring Kathryn Bernardo and Daniel Padilla. The film was shot in many locations in Cebu and Laguna in the Philippines. The film was produced by Star Cinema and was released nationwide on March 13, 2013. This marks the first solo film with love tandem Kathryn Bernardo and Daniel Padilla in a leading role following their roles in the ensemble films 24/7 in Love and Sisterakas in 2012. Main Cast: Kathryn Bernardo as Princess Patricia "Patchot" Espinosa Daniel Padilla as Ivan Lacson

She's Dating the Gangster
is a 2014 Philippine coming-of-age romantic comedy drama film based on the Pop Fiction book of the same name originally published on CandyMag.com's Teen Talk section and it was popularized on Wattpad by Bianca Bernardino (pen name: SGwannaB). The film is directed by Cathy Garcia-Molina, topbilled by Kathryn Bernardo and Daniel Padilla, together with Dawn Zulueta and Richard Gomez. It was distributed by Star Cinema with the co-production of Summit Media and was released on July 16, 2014, in theatres nationwide as part of its 20th anniversary presentation. The House Arrest of Us
is a 2020 Philippine romantic comedy streaming television series starring Kathryn Bernardo and Daniel Padilla, directed by Richard Arellano and written by Carmi Raymundo. It is about an engaged couple who get locked in one house with both their families.
